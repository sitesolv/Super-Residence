A Few Words About Us

Super Residence is more than just a place to stay; it’s where comfort and relaxation come together in perfect harmony. Tucked away in Sri Lanka’s stunning landscapes, our villa offers the peace of nature alongside modern luxury. Whether you’re here for a weekend getaway or a longer retreat, you’ll find everything you need to unwind and feel at home.

Enjoy a refreshing swim in our beautiful pool, surrounded by lush gardens, or spend your days exploring nearby beaches and local attractions. At Super Residence, we pride ourselves on creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere with personalized service to make your stay unforgettable.

Come as guests, leave as family. Super Residence – where your vacation becomes a home.
